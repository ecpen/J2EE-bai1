# Quan Ly Sach - Book Management System

## Mo ta
Chuong trinh quan ly sach bang Java, su dung console de tuong tac.

## Chuc nang
1. Them 1 cuon sach
2. Xoa 1 cuon sach
3. Thay doi (sua) 1 cuon sach
4. Xuat thong tin tat ca cac cuon sach
5. Tim cuon sach co tua de chua chu "Lap trinh" (khong phan biet hoa thuong)
6. Lay sach: Nhap vao 1 so K va gia P. Lay toi da K cuon sach co gia <= P
7. Nhap vao 1 danh sach cac tac gia tu ban phim. Cho biet tat ca cuon sach cua nhung tac gia nay
0. Thoat

## Cach chay
1. Compile: `javac src/main/java/*.java`
2. Run: `java -cp src/main/java BookManagement`
